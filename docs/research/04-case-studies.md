# Case Studies: Whale Bets and Suspected Insider Trades

## 2024 U.S. Election (Oct-Nov 2024)

- Four accounts staked >$30M on Trump, pushing odds to ~60% vs 40% Harris.
- Election Day volumes exceeded $3.5B.
- Drew speculation about bettors' identities and foreign influence on prices.
- **Relevant metrics**: Large trade size, profit concentration.

## Venezuela Leadership Change (Jan 2026)

- New Polymarket accounts collectively bet heavily that Maduro would be ousted by Jan 31.
- One account profited ~$400,000 when Maduro was unexpectedly captured.
- Lawmakers questioned if traders had insider knowledge of secret US operations.
- Bets timed hours before the outcome.
- **Relevant metrics**: New accounts, timing, large trade size.

## Iran Conflict Ceasefire (Apr 7, 2026)

- Dozens of freshly-created wallets placed large "Yes" bets on US-Iran ceasefire.
- One wager: ~$72,000 at $0.088/share, just ahead of surprise peace announcement.
- Traders cashed out hundreds of thousands in profit before most knew a deal was imminent.
- Polymarket flagged the market as "disputed" due to ceasefire definition ambiguity.
- **Relevant metrics**: New accounts, timing, coordinated activity, large trade size.

## War Bets Cluster (Late 2025 - Early 2026)

- Blockchain forensic firms identified linked wallets profiting from Middle East conflict
  bets.
- One group: 100% win rate across Iran attack predictions, earning $1.35M.
- Another set: correctly called multiple Iran surprise attacks, profiting $560,000.
- ~36 wallets made identical geopolitical bets, cashed out to one exchange address.
- **Relevant metrics**: High win rate, coordinated activity, new accounts, timing.

## Key Takeaways for Detection

1. Insider patterns typically trigger **multiple** detection metrics simultaneously.
2. Fresh wallets + perfect timing + large size is the strongest signal combination.
3. Fund-flow analysis (where profits go) can link otherwise anonymous wallets.
4. Not all flagged traders are proven insiders -- some may be well-informed analysts.
